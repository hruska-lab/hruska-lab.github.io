Hruška Lab

Computational pharmaceutical science

Research lab investigating drug-protein interactions with the help of high-throughput atomistic simulation and explainable machine learning.
Located at Faculty of Pharmacy, Charles University in Hradec Králové, Czech Republic.
Research results are described in section Research.
For news follow on Twitter.
